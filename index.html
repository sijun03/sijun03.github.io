Mnemox — Spaced Repetition, Powered by AI

Mnemox is a minimalist AI flashcard web app that helps you remember anything quickly, privately, and beautifully. It uses spaced repetition to boost long-term retention while keeping the interface fast, calm, and distraction-free.

AI Powered

Give Mnemox a deck name and short description, and it can generate relevant flashcards automatically.

Key Features

  • AI-generated flashcards from your deck title and description
  • Create and edit your own decks
  • Study only due cards each day
  • Rate your recall (Again, Hard, Good, Easy)
  • CSV portability
  • Optional card notes

Principles

  • Fast. Opens instantly and keeps you in flow.
  • Private. Your decks live with you.
  • Simple. Calm, minimalist UI.

Product Vision

  • One-liner: A minimalist AI flashcard web app to remember anything quickly, privately, and beautifully.
  • Problem: Most SRS apps feel bloated or lock data into proprietary clouds.
  • Who is this for: Self-learners, professionals, and students.
