Story behind the name

Codisoul — the name was born out of three threads woven together:
  • Code — the craft I practice, the language I think in.
  • Soul — my passion, the energy I pour into what I build.
  • Cortisol — the stress hormone. Codisoul reminds me that simple apps should ease human stress, not add to it.

The name is both a tribute to passion and a quiet rebellion — coding not just as logic, but as something that lives and breathes.
The hexagon emblem comes from the honeycomb of bees. It was inspired by a quote that struck me deeply:

When the world says you can’t, remember someone once said: by aerodynamics, a bee shouldn't fly.. But bees don’t know that — they fly.!

Like bees, Codisoul is about believing, staying tiny — yet flying high.

Spaced Cards

A minimalist flashcard app powered by spaced repetition.

Learn faster with short, focused sessions. Spaced Cards stays out of your way so you can build a habit without friction.

Principles

  • Fast. Opens instantly and keeps you in flow.
  • Private. Your decks live with you. No data selling.
  • Simple. Calm, minimalist UI with readable typography and minimal colors.

Product Vision

  • One-liner: A minimalist iPhone flashcard app that uses spaced repetition to remember anything quickly, privately, and beautifully.
  • Problem: Many SRS apps feel bloated or lock data in proprietary clouds. Users want something local-first, private, and fast.
  • Who is this for: Primary: self-learners and professionals who prefer distraction-free repetition. Secondary: students.

Value proposition

  • Gesture-driven speed: Fast, intuitive interactions that keep you focused.
  • Local-first privacy: Your data stays on your device, with no cloud lock-in.
  • Referral-based Cloud DB sync: Optional cloud sync that respects your privacy.
  • CSV portability: Easily import/export your decks.
  • Optional card notes: Add context to your cards without clutter.
