My Thinking

Build small, useful, respectful tools. Ship early. Improve often.

Codisoul is about crafting software that respects your time and your data. Fewer features, done well, matter more than long checklists. Privacy is a feature — not a toggle. I iterate quickly: sketch → wireframe → prototype → feedback → refine. The goal is calm, fast, beautiful tools that help you do more.

Spaced Cards

A minimalist flashcard app powered by spaced repetition.

Learn faster with short, focused sessions. Spaced Cards stays out of your way so you can build a habit without friction.

Principles

  • Fast. Opens instantly and keeps you in flow.
  • Private. Your decks live with you. No data selling.
  • Beautiful. Calm UI, readable type, accessible contrast.

What’s inside

  • Spaced repetition scheduling that just works
  • Quick card creation (Markdown-style)
  • Light/Dark theme, keyboard shortcuts
  • Local-first storage; export when you need it
